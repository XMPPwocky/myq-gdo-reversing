====== WifiModule ======
Created Wednesday 30 December 2020

Fn-Link 6220N-IS, based on [[WifiModule:RTL8721D]]


Has at least two UARTs - one for debugging, one for communication. The [[WifiModule:DebugUART]] is exposed on a header, and allows arbitrary memory and flash read/write.

Based around Realtek "Ameba-D" SoC.
